FRUIT NINJA VR
In this game we will make the fruits disappear when we click them and it increases the score.
If we click on the bomb among the fruits we will lose a heart among 3 hearts.
we will keep a scoreboard at the left hand side of the screen and 3 hearts on the right hand side.
if we lose all three hearts ,the game shows GAME OVER!! and the final score.
the home screen will show (YOUR NAME________)and (PLAY) options.
we will have 3 stages.start stage(before we hit play),play stage(in which we actually play)and end stage(it shows return to main menu and score with GAME OVER!! sweetalert)
GLTF MODELS REQUIRED--
Apple fruit model
Banana fruit model
Watermelon fruit model
Mango fruit model
Bomb model
